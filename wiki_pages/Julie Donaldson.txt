Julie Anne Donaldson (born February 10, 1978) is an American sportscaster and media producer for the Washington Football Team of the National Football League (NFL). Prior to that, she served as an anchor, host, and reporter for several television networks, and also won the 2001 Miss Florida USA beauty pageant. Donaldson became the first full-time on-air female member of an NFL broadcasting team upon her hiring by Washington in 2020.

La Verrerie (French: [la vɛʁ(ə)ʁi]; Arpitan: La Vèrriére [la vɛˈʁjeʁ(ə)] (listen) is a municipality in the district of Veveyse in the canton of Fribourg in Switzerland. It was formed on 1 January 2004 by the union of the municipalities of Le Crêt, Grattavache, and Progens. The municipality is administered from Le Crêt.

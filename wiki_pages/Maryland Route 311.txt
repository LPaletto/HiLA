Maryland Route 311 (MD 311) is a state highway in the U.S. state of Maryland.  Known for most of its length as Henderson Road, the state highway runs 6.11 miles (9.83 km) from MD 313 in Goldsboro north to MD 454 in Marydel in Caroline County.  MD 311 was constructed in the mid-1920s.  The state highway originally continued through Marydel to the Delaware state line, but was rolled back in favor of MD 454 in the mid-1940s.

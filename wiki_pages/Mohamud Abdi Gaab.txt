Mohamud Abdi Gaab (Somali: Maxamuud Cabdi Gaab) was the governor of Hiraan from 2011 to 2014.On 1 January 2012, Gaab arrived in the Kenyan capital, Nairobi, with an accompanying delegation from Uganda. During his time there, he met with various intellectuals, scholars and religious leaders of Hiraan.

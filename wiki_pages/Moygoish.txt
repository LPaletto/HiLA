Moygoish (Irish: Uí Mhac gCuais) is a barony in north County Westmeath, in the Republic of Ireland, formed by 1672. It is bordered by County Longford to the west and four other Westmeath baronies: Corkaree (to the east), Fore (to the north–east), Moyashel and Magheradernon (to the south–east) and Rathconrath to the south–west.

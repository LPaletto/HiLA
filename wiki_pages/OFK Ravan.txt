Omladinski fudbalski klub Ravan (Serbian Cyrillic: Омладински  фудбалски  клуб  Раван, English: Ravan Youth Football Club), commonly known as OFK Ravan, is a Bosnian football club from Kozarska Dubica, Republika Srpska, more precisely from a village Međeđa. They have played in the Second League of the Republika Srpska since 2003, but were relegated to the Regional League in 2012.

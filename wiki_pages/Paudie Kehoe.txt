Paudie Kehoe (born 1990) is an Irish hurler who plays as a left corner-forward for the Carlow senior team.Kehoe made his first appearance for the team during the 2009 Christy Ring Cup and has become a regular member of the starting fifteen since then. During that time he has won one Christy Ring Cup medal.
At club level Kehoe has won five county championship medal with St Mullin's.

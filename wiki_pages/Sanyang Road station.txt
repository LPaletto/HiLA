Sanyang Road Station (simplified Chinese: 三阳路; traditional Chinese: 三陽路; pinyin: sān yáng lù) serves as an interchange station of Line 1 and Line 7 of Wuhan Metro. The elevated station entered revenue service along with the completion of Line 1, Phase 1 on July 28, 2004, and the underground station entered service on October 1, 2018 with the opening of Line 7.

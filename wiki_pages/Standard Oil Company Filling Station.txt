The Standard Oil Company Filling Station at 638 College St. in Bowling Green, Kentucky was built in 1921.  It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2010.It was operated as a filling station until 1956.  The station was renovated and reopened as public restrooms for the adjacent Circus Square Park in 2009.The renovation included having reproduction Red Crown Gasoline globes made, funded by the Landmark Association.

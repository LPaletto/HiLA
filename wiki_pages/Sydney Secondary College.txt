Sydney Secondary College is a multi-campus urban government-funded, co-educational, dual modality, partially academically selective and comprehensive secondary day school, located in the Inner West region of Sydney, New South Wales, Australia.Established in 2002, the College's three campuses are located in the Sydney suburbs of Leichhardt, Balmain and Glebe and cater for approximately 2,000 students from Year 7 to Year 12.

Tibia is a 1997  massively multiplayer online role-playing game (MMORPG) developed and published by CipSoft.  It is one of the earliest and longest-running MMORPGs, with its popularity peaking in 2007. It is a free game to download and play, though players may pay to upgrade to a premium account, granting substantial in-game benefits. Tibia is a two-dimensional tile-based game set in a fantasy world with pixel art graphics and a top-down perspective.

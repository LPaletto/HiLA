Wola Starogrodzka [ˈvɔla starɔˈɡrɔt͡ska] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Parysów, within Garwolin County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) north-west of Parysów, 12 km (7 mi) north of Garwolin, and 48 km (30 mi) south-east of Warsaw.
The village has a population of 632.
